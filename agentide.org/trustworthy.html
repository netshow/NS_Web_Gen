Building Trust Through Transparency

Trust is at the core of our relationship with users. Discover the policies and practices that make Agent IDE Org a platform you can rely on with confidence.

Privacy-First Approach

Your data is yours. We ensure data usage transparency, provide user control over data (deletion, export), and practice minimal data retention, adhering strictly to our Privacy Policy.

Robust Security Measures

HTTPS/TLS encryption, data-at-rest encryption, strict internal access controls, regular security testing (vulnerability scanning, pen-testing), and 2FA support for account security.

Reliability & Accountability

High uptime commitment with redundancies, transparent incident communication, ownership of errors with post-mortems, and commitment to ethical AI practices and platform use.

Clear Communication

Honest marketing avoiding hype, clear documentation and support responses, and open communication about mistakes or unintended changes. We don't promise what we can't deliver.

Compliance & Standards

Adherence to major privacy laws (GDPR/CCPA), industry best practices for cloud security and uptime, and commitment to accessibility and inclusivity for all users.

Supportive Community

Nurturing an open forum for peer-to-peer support and transparent discussions. Publishing real user testimonials and protecting the community from abuse, fostering mutual respect.

Longevity & Stability Plans

Commitment to long-term product support, careful migration for feature changes, and financial soundness to ensure we're a reliable partner for the future.

Continuous Improvement in Trust

Regular review of policies and practices, adapting to new threats or regulations, and actively soliciting user feedback on trust-related aspects to ensure we meet community expectations.

Our Pledge of Trust

At Agent IDE Org, trustworthiness is not just a policy; it's ingrained in our culture and operations. We are committed to:

  • Prioritizing Your Privacy
  • Implementing Strong Security
  • Communicating Openly
  • Following Ethical Standards
  • Fostering a Respectful Community

We continuously ask, "If I were the user, what would build my trust?" and act accordingly. We want you to use AgentIDE with complete confidence, knowing your interests are protected every step of the way.
