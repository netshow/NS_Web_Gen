Disclosure

Our Commitment to Transparency and Integrity

At Agent IDE Org, we believe in being transparent with our users about our business practices, partnerships, and any factors that might influence the content or recommendations on our site. This Disclosure page is intended to clarify any financial relationships, partnerships, or other affiliations we have, ensuring you can trust the information and services we provide.

Partnerships and Affiliations

  • NetShow.AI: Agent IDE Org is proudly powered by NetShow.AI’s technology, and we maintain a close partnership with NetShow.AI. This means that we collaborate on technology development and integration. However, Agent IDE Org is an independent product and company. Our partnership does not compromise our commitment to serving our users’ best interests. We disclose this relationship openly because we want you to know that some of the AI backbone of Agent IDE Org is provided by NetShow.AI (a positive, quality-driven partnership). There are no hidden payments or sponsorship influencing our content – it’s a straightforward tech collaboration.
  • Cloud Services (e.g., Firebase): We utilize cloud infrastructure from providers like Google Firebase and others to host Agent IDE Org. We have no special financial deals with these providers beyond standard service agreements. We mention them as part of our commitment to using reliable, modern technology for our platform.
  • Other Tool Integrations: Agent IDE Org can integrate with many third-party APIs and services (like social media platforms, email services, etc.) for the purpose of agent functionality. We do not have financial partnerships with these external services for promoting them. Any service we integrate or mention (e.g., “connect your Slack or Gmail”) is purely because it’s useful for our users, not because we’re paid to do so.

Financial Model

Agent IDE Org currently operates on a subscription model (with a free tier available). Our revenue comes from users who choose paid plans for higher usage or enterprise features. We do not make money by selling user data or through advertising on our site. If this ever changes (for example, if we introduce a new revenue stream), we will update this disclosure and let users know.

We may at times promote our own premium features or related services, but we will always be clear that it’s our offering. If we ever highlight third-party products or services (for instance, on a blog or resources page), we will explicitly state if there’s any affiliate relationship.

Affiliate Links and Recommendations

As of now, the Agent IDE Org website and blog do not use affiliate links. Any product reviews or recommendations on our blog (for example, if we discuss “Top tools to use with AI agents”) are based on our genuine opinion and research. We are not paid to include any particular tool in those discussions. We aim to keep any educational content unbiased and purely value-driven.

In the future, if we were to include affiliate links (which provide a commission to us if you purchase something through the link), we will clearly label them as such with a note like “[Affiliate Link]” or a statement explaining the relationship. Transparency is key; we want you to trust that our content’s primary goal is to inform, not to secretly advertise.

Testimonials and Reviews

Our website includes testimonials and success stories from users of Agent IDE Org. These reflect the real experiences of those users. We have not paid or offered undue incentives for positive testimonials – though we may have offered things like beta access or discounts for early feedback, the words are their own. In cases where testimonials come from users who have a business relationship with us (e.g., a partner or someone who later became an affiliate), we will note that context next to their testimonial.

Similarly, any “Reviews” or case studies presented are truthful to the best of our knowledge. If pseudonyms or stock images are used for privacy reasons (sometimes small business users prefer not to publish their company name), we will mention that and assure the core story is real.

Editorial Independence

Our content (including blog articles, FAQs, guides) is written with the aim to educate and assist our users. We maintain editorial independence, meaning our partnership with NetShow.AI or any other entity does not dictate what we say. If we mention NetShow or any partner in content, it’s because it’s relevant and useful, not because they asked us to.

In fact, we often reference external sources and industry data to provide unbiased context. This approach ensures that we’re providing helpful, reliable information rather than marketing fluff.

Conflict of Interest

We affirm that to the best of our knowledge, we do not have any conflicts of interest that could affect the integrity of our website content or service:

  • We do not have investors or parent companies that pressure us to favor certain content or integrations that aren’t in user interests.
  • Our employees are required to disclose any personal conflicts (e.g., if someone has stock in a tool company we integrate) and we manage those to avoid bias.
  • If any potential conflict arises, we will either avoid the situation or openly disclose it to our users.

Changes to This Disclosure

If anything in our financial relationships or partnerships changes in a way that would be relevant to disclose, we will update this page accordingly. We will also notify our users if a major change occurs (for example, if we were acquired by another company or if we started any form of advertising on the platform).

Our goal is to ensure you always understand who you’re dealing with and why we make the recommendations we do. The trust of our user community is paramount, and we’ll continue to earn that by being upfront and honest in all aspects of our operations.

If you have any questions about our disclosures or something you’ve seen on the site that you want clarity on, please contact us. We’ll be happy to explain or discuss any concerns.
